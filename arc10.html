Arc 10 — Exfil and Testimony

GM — Phase I Recap (10 Bullets)
  • Arrival: PCs entered Haven under Accord mandate amid outages and factional tension.
  • Helion: Governor Hestrel pressed calm for the Board; Veyne tested the Accord’s deniable assets.
  • Reclaimers: Serin Vael and Rust Varo pressed dignity, risking everything to trust outsiders.
  • Zeratek: Kaelen Drix angled to bury emitter faults, trading fixes with blackmail clauses.
  • Compliance: Director Sael offered sedation in exchange for quiet. “Screenings” as stability.
  • Accord: Saint demanded receipts only truth could carry, refusing optics or spin.
  • The Choir: Variances hummed, wires sang. Miners whispered the Choir and would not repeat it.
  • Silent Guide: Ren led PCs with burned modules and variance logs, throat ruined but message intact.
  • The Crawl: Dampeners spent, sparks endured, proof carried through veins of Haven’s Factory.
  • Final Stakes: PCs escaped with evidence; factions now circle, truth burning hotter than power surges.

Mandate: leave the Factory with proof intact, decide who receives it first, and survive the spin war. Timers: Audit Window, Helion Response, Compliance Sweep.

Scene 1 — The Stair That Chooses You

“Truth arrives late to meetings but ruins the agenda.” — Saint

The Factory groaned as you left its underbelly. Stairs, ramps, and lifts shifted, rearranging themselves like bureaucrats drafting a budget: patient, deliberate, cruel. What should have been a straightforward exfiltration became a ritual of choices, each stairwell painted with allegiance.

The north stair gleamed in municipal gray, the paint almost honest. The east liftsouth ramp

GM — Exit Fork
  • North Stair (Helion Optics): Clean optics, harder testimony. +1 Helion Stability.
  • East Lift (Compliance): Fast but flagged. Save vs. Spells or −2 to first reaction roll. +1 Compliance Control.
  • South Ramp (Accord Streets): Gritty, riskier now, stronger later. +1 Reclaimer Trust, +2 if Ren visible.

Note: If Audit Window ≤ 1h, trigger Internal Sweep (2 drones + 1 Compliance team).

Scene 2 — Who Gets the Truth

“Testimony is a weapon that breaks in the hand that lies.” — Reclaimer proverb

The proof weighed more than its fragments. Burned modules, variance logs, and the Icon of Compliance—any one could topple careers or spark riots. All together, they promised a story Haven would not survive unscarred. But first, someone must own that story.

Helion’s lobby awaited in sterile light, screens ready to broadcast calm. The Accord waited in a quiet room, Saint ready with ink instead of cameras. The Reclaimers gathered in Graywater, the street murmuring for justice.

GM — Delivery Fork
  • Helion: Gain Stability; lose Accord Optics. PCs framed as calm escorts. Helion holds narrative.
  • Accord: Gain Optics; Accord watches but makes PCs prove it in hearings. Helion Stability −1.
  • Street: Reclaimer Trust +2; Compliance sweeps intensify. Accord influence limited.

Ren’s presence amplifies whichever delivery he accompanies—hero, liability, or martyr.

Scene 3 — The Choir’s Invitation

“When a machine sings without power, ask not what debt was paid.” — Accord field notes

The evidence handed off, the Choir did not retreat. Lights bent, wires whispered, aurora stitched across the dome’s skin. The city itself began to sing—a hymn or a warning. Every choice echoed, not in optics, but in resonance.

One by one, familiar figures turned their faces upward: Veyne at the Spire steps, Saint beneath dim lamps, Serin in Graywater. All heard the same note. Haven itself was being tuned.

GM — Choir Contact
  • Save vs. Spells: Fail = temporary surrender, +1 Choir Influence. Success = resist the voice.
  • Lira Veyne: If PCs resist, she offers to hunt Choir roots. If they join, she marks them as lost.
  • Saint: Demands Accord doctrine be read against the song. PCs may anchor or fracture his faith.
  • Serin/Rust: If PCs join, they witness betrayal; if PCs resist, they become bulwarks for Graywater.

Phase II Bridge — After the First Song

Phase I concludes. Proof lodged, Choir awakened. Phase II begins in the echo.

GM — Final Clocks & Flags
  • Accord Optics ≥ +4: Accord dominates hearings. PCs gain Press Leverage.
  • Helion Stability ≤ −2: Board intervention. PCs gain Access: Spire.
  • Compliance Control ≥ +3: Curfews & sweeps. PCs flagged; Heat starts at 1 in all Spire tiers.
  • Reclaimer Trust ≥ +3: Graywater shelters PCs. Serin offers strike leverage.
  • Zeratek Favor high/low: Drix becomes ally or saboteur. Firmware chain unlocked or poisoned.

Carry Ren forward if alive; if lost, Phase II opens with a Rescue or Requiem.
