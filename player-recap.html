Phase I Recap — What You Know

  • You arrived at Haven Spire under an Accord mandate, told only: stabilize the situation.
  • Governor Orus Hestrel promised stability, but seemed more worried about appearances than truth.
  • Captain Lira Veyne earned your respect—direct, principled, and always in the line herself.
  • The Reclaimers, led by Serin Vael and Rust Varo, fight for safer quarters and basic dignity.
  • Zeratek’s liaison, Kaelen Drix, smiles through excuses and pushes faulty tech as if it’s favor.
  • Director Sael of Compliance Medicine offers “relief” that looks a lot like sedation.
  • Saint, your Accord handler, only trusts proof—documents, film, recordings, anything unspinnable.
  • Strange electrical surges whisper rhythms in ducts and wires. Workers call it the Choir.
  • Ren, a miner with his voice taken, showed you where the Choir and policy overlap in the Factory.
  • You carry proof, scars, and choices. Haven waits to see who you trust with the truth.
